Questions

Good to know

Is lucid dreaming actually real?+
Yes. It's a studied state first verified in a sleep lab in 1975, where a dreaming volunteer signaled awareness using pre-agreed eye movements while still in REM. About half of people have had a lucid dream at least once by accident — HalfLight just helps you do it on purpose.
Do I need the AI features to get value?+
Not at all. The journal, lucid lessons, streaks, and progress tracking are completely free. The AI interpretation, auto-tagging, and title generation are the Pro additions for dreamers who want extra insight.
How private are my dreams?+
Your dreams live on your device first and sync to a private, row-level-secured space only when you sign in. AI requests run through a secure backend — the API keys never ship in the app, and AI only runs when you ask it to.
Can I use HalfLight without an account?+
Yes — you can journal locally right away. Signing in with email or Apple adds cloud backup and sync across devices. You can also permanently delete your account and data from inside the app at any time.
What platforms are supported?+
HalfLight is a native iOS app built with SwiftUI, including a home-screen widget. iPhone is the primary experience today.
